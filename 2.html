Watashi no mono:

Land of the roses

This is not a remake or remaster of Watashi no Mono original, this is merely a spin off with permission by epic-meal dev to use any of the character's and it's world. created by fans.

This is an semi-open world game that takes place in the Watashi no Mono universe.

> Main Character: Uchikina Kurutta

After a devastating fire destroyed the last school, she went with her boy friend to a over sea's international school in england to leave behind her hideous self ...or did she?
